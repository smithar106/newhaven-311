📬 How it works: When a ticket is submitted under a category that has a responsible email set, that person immediately receives an email with the tracking number, location, description, and a link to the ticket — so nothing falls through the cracks.
⚙️ Email requires SMTP configuration (Railway environment variables): Set SMTP_USER, SMTP_PASS, SMTP_HOST (default: smtp.gmail.com), and SMTP_PORT (default: 587) in your Railway service variables. For Gmail, use an App Password. If SMTP is not configured, routing emails are silently skipped.
