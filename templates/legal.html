1. Introduction

MyCity311 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the MyCity311 mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this policy carefully. By using MyCity311, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Report content — descriptions of issues, category selections, and any details you enter when submitting a complaint
  • Photos and media — images you choose to attach to a report
  • Contact information — name and email address if you voluntarily provide them for report tracking purposes
  • Tracking number requests — if you opt in to receive a tracking number via email

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location data — GPS coordinates at the time of report submission, used to pin the location of the reported issue. We request location access only when you submit a report.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage data — pages viewed, features used, time spent in the app, and crash reports
  • IP address — collected automatically when you access the Service

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not collect Social Security numbers or government ID
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and display your submitted reports
  • Generate and deliver tracking numbers for your submissions
  • Improve the functionality and performance of the Service
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance the user experience
  • Communicate with you regarding your reports (if you provided contact information)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse of the Service

4. How We Share Your Information

Reports Are Publicly Visible

Submitted reports, including their category, description, location, and any attached photos, may be visible to other users of the Service and to the general public. Do not include sensitive personal information in your report descriptions.

Service Providers

We may share information with third-party vendors who assist us in operating the Service, including cloud hosting providers and analytics services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may not use it for any other purpose.

City and Government Partners

Where we have established agreements with city governments or municipal agencies, we may share report data with those partners for the purpose of addressing the reported issues. We will disclose which cities are active partners within the app.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, or investigate fraud.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Location Data

The Service requests access to your device's location services to automatically populate the location of a reported issue. Location access is requested only at the time of report submission. You may deny location access and enter a location manually. We do not track your location continuously or in the background.

6. Photos and Camera Access

The Service requests camera and photo library access only when you choose to attach a photo to a report. We do not access your camera or photo library at any other time.

7. Data Retention

We retain submitted reports and associated data for a period of up to 3 years from the date of submission, after which it is deleted or anonymized. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@mycity311.co.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — opt out of any non-essential data collection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mycity311.co.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information. To submit a CCPA request, contact privacy@mycity311.co.

European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation including the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of your data. Our legal basis for processing is your consent and our legitimate interest in operating the Service.

9. Children's Privacy

MyCity311 is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@mycity311.co and we will delete it promptly.

10. Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Third-Party Links and Services

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
